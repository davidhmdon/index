Green Sempervivum (set of 6)

Six plants in a selection of green varieties.

£12.99



A set of six sempervivum plants from our collection, patterned in various shades of green. You may find some tipped with red or deep purple in the summer, but they are predominantly green.

These perseverant plants - sometimes called houseleeks - are named for how they carry their leaves through winter and resist the effects of harsh environments. Semper (always) vivum (living) are aptly titled - strong plants whose leaves grow into pretty rosettes.

They will grow almost anywhere and with the minimum of attention. All our plants are named varieties and we send them still growing in their pots.
